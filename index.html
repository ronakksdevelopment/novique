Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out and our team will get back to you within one business day.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines depend on scope: a marketing site typically takes 2 to 4 weeks, while a full product design and development engagement runs 8 to 16 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline after our discovery call.

Do you work with startups or only enterprise clients?

Both. Our Starter and Growth plans are built for early-stage and scaling companies, while our Premium tier serves enterprise teams needing dedicated resourcing and custom software.

What does the payment structure look like?

Most engagements are split into three milestones: 40% at kickoff, 30% at design approval, and 30% at launch. Retainer and monthly billing options are available for ongoing partnerships.

Will I own the final designs and code?

Absolutely. Once final payment is received, full ownership of all design files, source code and assets transfers to you: no licensing fees, no lock-in.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes, every plan includes a support window post-launch, and we offer ongoing retainer packages for ongoing iteration, monitoring and optimization work.

Can you work with our existing in-house team?

Definitely. We regularly collaborate alongside internal design, product and engineering teams, either leading a workstream or supporting yours as an extension of it.
